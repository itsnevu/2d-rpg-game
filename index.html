HOW TO PLAY

Get started in Near Lands in 3 simple steps

1

Connect Wallet

Click "Login with Solana" and sign in with your Phantom or any Solana wallet. Your wallet is your game identity — no username/password needed.

2

Enter the World

Once connected, set your nickname and select a character. The world is a persistent open map shared with all players in real-time.

3

Survive & Explore

Collect resources, craft weapons and shelters, and interact with other players. Manage your HP, hunger, thirst, and warmth to survive.

4

Controls

Move with WASD or Arrow Keys. Open inventory with E. Interact with F. Use hotbar slots 1-9. On mobile, use the on-screen joystick and buttons.
